About

I’m Paola 👋, an aspiring UX/UI designer with passion for projects that spark delight. As an Interactive Arts and Technology student specializing in Design at SFU, I create interfaces and digital graphics that reflect my love for everything colourful and curious.

Way back when, I used to collect department store flyers as a kid and kept the ones I thought were too pretty to get recycled. An unusual origin, but this is where my love for design began. Today, I’m inspired by subtle details, retro design, and the art of emotion.🌀
